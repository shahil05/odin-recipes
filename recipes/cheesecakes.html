Cheesecakes

Description

This cheesecake is rich, creamy, and perfect for dessert lovers. It’s easy to make and a crowd favorite!

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Graham cracker crust

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add eggs one at a time and blend.
  4. Pour into crust and bake for 45 minutes.
