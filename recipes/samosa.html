Samosa

Description

Samosa is a popular Indian snack made with a crispy pastry filled with spicy potato and peas. It's deep-fried and served hot with chutney.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • Green peas
  • Green chilies
  • Garam masala
  • Carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt
  • Oil (for frying and kneading)

Steps

  1. Prepare dough with flour, ajwain, salt, and oil. Let it rest.
  2. Make the filling by saut√©ing mashed potatoes, peas, spices, and chilies.
  3. Divide dough into balls, roll and cut into halves.
  4. Shape into cones, fill with potato mixture, and seal.
  5. Fry samosas on medium heat until golden and crisp.
