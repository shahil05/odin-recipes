Gulab Jamun

Description

Gulab Jamun is a soft, spongy Indian dessert soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup. It melts in the mouth and is often served warm or cold.

Ingredients

  • Khoya (mawa)
  • Maida (all-purpose flour)
  • Milk or water (for kneading)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil (for frying)
  • Sugar
  • Rose water
  • Water (for syrup)

Steps

  1. Mix khoya, maida, and a little milk to form a soft dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small smooth balls.
  3. Fry the balls on low-medium heat until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with water, sugar, and rose water.
  5. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
